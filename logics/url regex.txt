______________________________________________________________

Regex para interpretar urls
______________________________________________________________

Url absoluta:
(?<scheme>http|https)://(?<host>[a-zA-Z.-]+)(?:(?<path>([^/?#]*?/)+)(?<file>[^/?#]*?)?(?<goto>#[^/?#]*?)?(?<query>\\?.*?)?$|$)

Url relativa:
(?<path>([^/?#]*?/)+)?(?<file>[^/?#]*?)?(?<goto>#[^/?#]*?)?(?<query>\\?.*?)?$

Detector de path:
[^/?#]*?/|(\\.\\.$)
